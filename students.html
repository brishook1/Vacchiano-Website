Michael Meckna

This list is derived from the following sources: The Juilliard School Archives, Manhattan School of Music Archives, “William Vacchiano, An Appreciative Recollection on His 83rd Birthday: 23 May 1995” by André M. Smith (ITG Journal, May 1995), “Tribute to Bill” by Ed Treutel, and Vacchiano’s method books Trumpet Routines and Moving Transposition, as well as Vacchiano’s “Personal Notes and Memoirs” collected and edited by Brian Shook.
